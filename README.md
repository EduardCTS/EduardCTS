I am a Computational Engineer and DeSci builder focused on structural biology and molecular dynamics, I build computational models and run in silico validations to solve structural bottlenecks in human longevity and biogerontology.

My current focus is deeply rooted in molecular dynamics using OpenMM, AMBER, and GROMACS, I specifically investigate Intrinsically Disordered Proteins (IDRs) and liquid-liquid phase separation. I am also working on translating extreme longevity mechanisms, such as those found in the bowhead whale, into human physiological conditions.
